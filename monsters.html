Bunker Spider

They are territorial ambush creatures, and when an Employee touches one of their webs or gets too close, they become hostile and start attacking.

Bracken

They are vicious predators capable of treading in near silence, constantly hunting down the closest player to them. If not careful, Brackens can easily sneak up behind a player to snap their neck and drag them away from the scene. However, if looked at, they get shy and quickly scurry away, but if they are looked at for too long, they become highly aggressive.

More Details
Coil-Head

They are eyeless mannequins with agape mouths, no forearms and a flexible spring for a neck. Their bodies are punctured with nails that are surrounded by what seem to be stains of blood. Coil-Heads are difficult to counter when alone as they must always be looked at to avoid potential death, more especially if you're dealing with another harmful entity at the same time.

More Details
Nutcracker

As the name implies, they are large nutcracker soldier figurines that are typically on the move. They roam until they find an Employee. They will scan the area for any movement, and if the Employee is seen moving, they will be shot at. When Nutcrackers are killed, they drop a double-barreled shotgun, which can be loaded, and two guaranteed Shotgun Shells upon death.

More Details
Barber

These faceless creatures follow a Master Barber's beat to move and attack players, jumping forward on a snare hit and slicing in half anything that collide with their scissors. They are the only entities that are typically invisible when far away, but are still able to be seen by all players when they get close to a player unlike the Ghost Girl, which can only be seen by one player.

More Details
Snare Flea

They use their silk to stick on ceilings and wait there patiently for their next kill. When an Employee walks under it or near it, they will drop down and wrap themselves on the Employee's head to suffocate them.
